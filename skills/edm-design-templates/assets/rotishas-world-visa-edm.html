Quick Tips

3 documents that get rejected most often

1

Bank statements older than 3 months

Most embassies want statements from the last 3 months specifically. An older statement — even if it shows a healthy balance — is grounds for rejection or a request for additional documents.

2

Invitation letters without host details

An invitation letter must include the host's full name, address, phone, passport or ID number, and a statement of responsibility. Generic letters from hotels rarely satisfy this.

3

Travel insurance with gaps in coverage

Schengen requires €30,000 minimum medical coverage for the entire trip duration. If your policy has a 24-hour exclusion window or country exclusions, it will fail the check.
