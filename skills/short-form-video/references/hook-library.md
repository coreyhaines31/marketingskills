# Video Hook Library

A comprehensive collection of proven hook formulas for short-form video content.

## Curiosity Hooks

### The "Secret" Formula
- "The secret to [outcome] that nobody talks about"
- "What [successful people] don't want you to know about [topic]"
- "I found the hidden feature in [product/platform] that changes everything"

### The Unexpected Discovery
- "I tried [thing] for 30 days and I was NOT expecting this"
- "I can't believe this actually worked..."
- "This completely changed how I think about [topic]"

### The Tease
- "This is the [thing] that [impressive result]"
- "Wait until you see what happens at the end"
- "What I'm about to show you might shock you"

### The Question
- "Why does nobody talk about this?"
- "Am I the only one who didn't know this?"
- "Has anyone else noticed [observation]?"

---

## Value Hooks

### The Promise
- "How to [achieve outcome] in [specific timeframe]"
- "The only guide you need for [topic]"
- "[Number] [things] that will change your [area of life]"
- "Everything you need to know about [topic] in 60 seconds"

### The Hack/Shortcut
- "[Outcome] hack that actually works"
- "The lazy way to [desirable outcome]"
- "Skip the hard part: here's how to [outcome]"
- "[Topic] cheat code: [brief description]"

### The Warning
- "Stop doing [common practice] — here's why"
- "[Number] mistakes that are killing your [results]"
- "Why [thing you think is good] is actually hurting you"
- "If you're doing [thing], stop immediately"

### The Comparison
- "[Method A] vs [Method B] — which actually works?"
- "I tested [multiple things] so you don't have to"
- "The difference between [beginner approach] and [expert approach]"

---

## Story Hooks

### The Transformation
- "3 months ago, I [bad state]. Today, I [good state]."
- "Here's how I went from [before] to [after]"
- "I used to think [old belief]. Then [event] changed everything."

### The Failure
- "I lost [something significant] because of this mistake"
- "Here's my biggest failure (and what I learned)"
- "I was completely wrong about [topic]"

### The Journey
- "So this just happened..."
- "You won't believe what happened when I tried [thing]"
- "Let me tell you about the time I [interesting situation]"

### The Confession
- "I need to come clean about something"
- "I've been lying about [topic] — here's the truth"
- "Something I've never shared before..."

---

## Authority Hooks

### The Credentials
- "After [impressive metric] doing [activity], here's what I learned"
- "I've [impressive experience] and this is the #1 thing that matters"
- "As someone who [relevant experience], let me tell you..."

### The Results
- "This one change got me [impressive result]"
- "How I [achieved impressive metric] in [timeframe]"
- "The exact strategy that [impressive outcome]"

### The Behind-the-Scenes
- "How [successful company/person] actually does [thing]"
- "The real reason [successful entity] is winning"
- "What [industry/company] doesn't want you to know"

---

## Controversy Hooks

### The Hot Take
- "Unpopular opinion: [bold statement]"
- "[Popular thing] is actually overrated"
- "I'm probably going to get hate for this, but..."
- "[Common practice] is a waste of time"

### The Myth Buster
- "[Common belief] is a myth. Here's the truth."
- "Everything you've been told about [topic] is wrong"
- "Stop believing this lie about [topic]"

### The Debate
- "[Thing A] is better than [Thing B] and it's not even close"
- "Let's settle this once and for all: [debate topic]"
- "The [topic] debate is over. Here's why."

## Relatable Hooks

### The "POV"
- "POV: You finally figured out [relatable situation]"
- "POV: You're a [role] dealing with [relatable problem]"
- "POV: When [relatable situation happens]"

### The "When"
- "When you realize [relatable realization]"
- "When [person/thing] says [relatable trigger]"
- "That moment when [relatable situation]"

### The Call-Out
- "This is for my [specific audience] who [relatable situation]"
- "If you're struggling with [problem], this is for you"
- "Tag someone who needs to see this"

### The Adaptation
- "[Trending topic] but make it [your niche]"
- "Applying [viral concept] to [your topic]"
- "The [viral trend] but for [your audience]"

### The Commentary
- "My thoughts on [trending topic]"
- "Let's talk about [viral thing]"
- "Everyone's getting [trending topic] wrong"

## Visual Hooks (First Frame)

### Text Overlays That Stop Scrolls
- "This changed my life"
- "Watch until the end"
- "I was today years old when I learned..."
- "Wait for it..."
- "This is insane"
- "FREE [valuable thing]"
- "[Shocking number or stat]"

### Visual Patterns
- Before/after split screen
- Circle or arrow pointing to something
- Reaction face (shock, confusion, excitement)
- Product/result shown immediately
- Movement/action in progress
- Unusual or unexpected visual

## Hook Combinations

The most effective videos combine multiple hook types:

### Curiosity + Value
"I can't believe nobody talks about this [topic] hack"

### Story + Transformation
"3 months ago I couldn't [thing]. Here's the one change that fixed everything."

### Controversy + Authority
"After 10 years in [industry], I can tell you that [common advice] is completely wrong"

### Relatable + Value
"If you're struggling with [problem], here are 5 things that actually helped me"

## Platform-Specific Considerations

### TikTok
- Trend-jacking hooks perform well
- Controversial takes get pushed
- Native/raw aesthetic preferred

### Instagram Reels
- Polished production can work
- Value/educational hooks strong
- Share-worthy hooks (tag someone)

### YouTube Shorts
- Searchable/SEO-friendly hooks
- Tutorial/how-to hooks perform well
- Longer context acceptable (60 sec)

---

## Quick Hook Checklist

Before publishing, verify your hook:

- [ ] Creates curiosity or promises clear value
- [ ] Works without sound (text overlay)
- [ ] First frame is visually interesting
- [ ] Hits in under 3 seconds
- [ ] Speaks directly to target audience
- [ ] Matches the content that follows
