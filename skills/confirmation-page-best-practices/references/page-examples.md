# Confirmation Page Examples: Detailed Breakdowns

## 1. Master Internet Marketing (Jeremy Haynes)
**URL:** masterinternetmarketing.com/mim-call-confirmed

**What it does well:**
- Opens with a multi-chapter main video covering welcome, breakout video instructions, sales team contact info, and expectations
- Features an "AI Jeremy" breakout — a decade of training data available 24/7 for pre-call questions (innovative use of AI to handle infinite scale)
- Breakout videos organized by exact topic: how the program works, niche fit, pricing, and "who this is NOT for" (radical transparency)
- Pricing breakout explicitly addresses who should NOT buy — filters out bad fits before the call
- Heavy emphasis on the community size and global reach to establish legitimacy

**Key lesson:** Naming who your program is NOT for builds more trust than only promoting who it IS for.

## 3. Ecom Boss (James Conti)
**URL:** ecombossmentoring.com.au/confirmed-call

**What it does well:**
- 8 specific breakout videos with exact time-stamped lengths (signals respect for viewer's time)
- Each breakout title is framed as a first-person question ("'How Will I Make Money?'" not "How You'll Make Money")
- Explicit Trustpilot callout with explanation of why Trustpilot is credible: *"businesses cannot delete or hide reviews — ensuring transparent, unfiltered feedback"*
- "Bonus Resources" section with full client case study videos (18–34 min) for serious prospects doing deep research
- Instagram link to founder's results reels — years of documented client wins

**Key lesson:** Explain *why* a review platform is trustworthy, not just link to it. And offer long-form content for the deeply skeptical.

## Cross-Page Patterns (What All High-Performers Share)

1. **No generic "thanks" language** — every page immediately pivots to value
2. **Video-first** — text alone doesn't build trust; video does
3. **First-person framing** on breakout questions mirrors internal dialogue
4. **Specific numbers** — show rates, results, dispute rates, subscriber counts
5. **Multiple layers** — main video → breakouts → testimonials → checklist
6. **Clear next steps** — calendar, community, review content, be ready to decide
7. **Transparent about who it's NOT for** — the pages that do this best perform best
