## Weibo Platform Overview

### Key Stats (2026)
- Monthly Active Users: 2.6亿+
- Core Demographics: 18-35岁，娱乐追星为主
- Content Format: 图文、话题讨论、短视频
- Key Feature: 热搜效应，话题传播

### Weibo vs Other Platforms
| Dimension | Weibo | WeChat | Douyin |
|-----------|-------|--------|--------|
| Content Lifespan | 24-48 hours | Days-weeks | Hours-days |
| Discovery | Hashtag, search | Social graph | Algorithm |
| Engagement | Public comments | Private chats | Likes, comments |
| Virality | High (hot search) | Medium (sharing) | High (algorithm) |

## Hot Search Strategy

### Hot Search Types
| Type | Cost | Duration | Best For |
|------|------|----------|----------|
| Natural | Free | 1-4 hours | Brand events, news |
| Paid | ¥100k+ | 4-24 hours | Product launches, campaigns |
| Seed | ¥10k-50k | 2-6 hours | Initial momentum |

### Hot Search Optimization
1. **Timing**: Post during peak hours (12:00-14:00, 20:00-23:00)
2. **Hashtag**: Create unique, memorable hashtag
3. **Content**: Controversial, emotional, or surprising
4. **KOL Support**: Coordinate with KOLs for initial buzz
5. **Engagement**: Respond to comments quickly

### Hot Search Content Formula
```
Hook (1st sentence) → Conflict/Emotion → Brand Connection → CTA
```

## Hashtag Campaign Strategy

### Campaign Types
| Type | Goal | Example |
|------|------|---------|
| Brand | Awareness | #品牌名新品发布# |
| Event | Engagement | #品牌活动# |
| Challenge | UGC | #品牌挑战赛# |
| Social | Conversation | #社会话题# |

### Hashtag Best Practices
1. **Length**: 4-8 characters ideal
2. **Memorability**: Easy to type and remember
3. **Uniqueness**: Not already in use
4. **Relevance**: Related to campaign goal
5. **Visual**: Include in images/videos

### Campaign Execution
```
Pre-launch (3-7 days):
- KOL seeding
- Content preparation
- Community notification

Launch (Day 1-3):
- Hot search push
- KOL amplification
- UGC incentive

Sustain (Day 4-7):
- User content curation
- Engagement maintenance
- Secondary pushes

Legacy (Day 8+):
- Content archive
- Case study
- Learnings
```

## Content Strategy

### Content Types
| Type | Engagement | Virality |
|------|------------|----------|
| Polls | High | Medium |
| Questions | High | High |
| Images | Medium | Medium |
| Videos | High | High |
| Long-form | Low | Low |

### Content Calendar
- **Daily**: 1-2 posts, mix of types
- **Weekly**: 1 interactive post (poll/question)
- **Monthly**: 1 campaign or event

### Posting Best Practices
1. **Frequency**: 1-3 posts per day
2. **Timing**: Peak hours (12:00-14:00, 20:00-23:00)
3. **Hashtags**: 2-3 relevant hashtags per post
4. **Images**: 1-9 images, optimized for mobile
5. **Video**: Under 1 minute for feed, 3-5 minutes for深度

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Forced virality**: Content doesn't feel natural
2. **Ignoring comments**: Not engaging with audience
3. **Over-promotion**: Too salesy content
4. **Bad timing**: Missing peak hours
5. **No follow-up**: Not converting attention to action
