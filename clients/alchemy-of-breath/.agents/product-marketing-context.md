## What They Do

Alchemy of Breath is a breathwork training school and community that offers:

1. **Breathwork Facilitator Training** — 8-month online (400-hour) and 4-month hybrid (with 3 weeks live at ASHA, Tuscany)
2. **BreathCamps** — Immersive 7-day retreats in Tuscany and online
3. **Free Sessions** — Weekly free online breathwork sessions (Sundays)
4. **Courses** — The Alchemist: Inner Journey, Alchemy Meditation, Bridge, Quantum Shift, Breath Secrets
5. **Shop** — Subscription content access, on-demand courses
6. **Facilitator Directory** — Find a certified AoB breathwork facilitator
