Everything you need to know

ChatGPT and Claude are general-purpose assistants — you have to prompt them from scratch every time, and they have no memory of your product, audience, or goals. Magista is purpose-built for marketing. Each agent is pre-trained on the best practices for its specific discipline (CRO, SEO, copywriting, etc.), reads your product-marketing-context automatically, and maintains a workflow across tasks. It's the difference between hiring a generalist temp and having a specialist who already knows your business.
A marketing skill is a structured set of instructions, frameworks, and best practices for a specific marketing discipline. Think of it as a playbook that guides how the agent thinks, what it checks, and how it delivers outputs. For example, the CRO skill instructs the agent to analyze pages across 12 conversion dimensions, prioritize by impact, and format output as testable hypotheses. Agents load the relevant skill before every task.
No. Magista is a no-code platform. You fill out your product-marketing-context (a guided form), activate the agents you want, and review outputs in your dashboard. For advanced features like analytics integration or A/B test automation, we provide simple one-click integrations with tools like Google Analytics, Segment, and your email platform. No engineering required.
Your product-marketing-context is a structured document you fill out once. It captures your product description, target audience, key differentiators, messaging pillars, competitors, tone of voice, and current goals. Every Magista agent reads this context before it acts, so outputs are always grounded in your specific business — not generic marketing copy. You can update it anytime, and all agents pick up the changes automatically.
