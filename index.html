Is IV therapy safe on my wedding day? +

Entirely. Every NÉVUE session is administered by a licensed registered nurse and overseen by a board-certified physician. We complete a brief health intake before every appointment, and our nurses are trained specifically for mobile, event-day settings. Sessions take 45–60 minutes and clients are photo-ready immediately after.

Will I bruise before the ceremony? +

Bruising is our highest priority — which is why we developed our No-Bruise Strategy. Before any needle is placed, your nurse uses advanced vein mapping technology to identify the optimal site. Our first-attempt success rate is exceptionally high, and in the rare event of any mark, our nurses carry arnica protocol to address it immediately. The majority of our brides are in front of a camera within the hour.

How far in advance should I book? +

We recommend securing your date at least six months before your wedding — earlier for peak season (April–June, September–November). Because NÉVUE is a concierge service, each nurse serves only one bridal party per day. Founding bride waitlist members receive priority access before dates open to the public.

Can my whole bridal party receive treatment? +

Yes. NÉVUE specializes in bridal party packages — bridesmaids, mothers of the bride, and even groomsmen. Groups of four or more receive dedicated concierge planning to stagger sessions so everyone is ready before the first look. Tell us your party size when you join the waitlist and we'll build around your timeline.
